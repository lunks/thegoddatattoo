GODA TATOO ARTIST

GODA is Gonzalo Damian Lopez was already in the early age of 19 years old a professional tatoo artist, comming from Montevideo-Uruguay he did beggin with his studio there and than move to Rio de Janeiro-Brazil where he still working until now a days. Gonzalo is member of several Tatoo Conventions in all over Latin America and Europe.

After working in Latin America for 15 years GODA is moving to Europe and has been in Spain, Germany, Sweeden , Denmark, England, etc.
